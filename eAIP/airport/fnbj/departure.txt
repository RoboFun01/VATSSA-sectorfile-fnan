===== FNBJ Departure =====

  * FNBJ doesn't have a dedicated Departure Position, with Dr. Antonio Agostinho Neto Approach (FNBJ APP) assuming the role of Departure.


==== Departure Procedures ====

  * After departure, maintain aircraft on runway heading. You may issue further climb instructions (usually FL150).
