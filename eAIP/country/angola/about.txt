==== Angola Information ====

Angola, officially the Republic of Angola, is a country located on the west coast of Southern Africa. It is the second-largest Lusophone (Portuguese-speaking) country in both total area and population (behind Brazil in both cases), and is the seventh-largest country in Africa. It is bordered by [[country:namibia:home|Namibia]] to the south, the [[country:congo-dr:home|Democratic Republic of the Congo]] to the north, [[country:zambia:home|Zambia]] to the east, and the Atlantic Ocean to the west. Angola has an exclave province, the province of Cabinda, that borders the [[country:congo:home|Republic of the Congo]] and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The capital and most populous city is Luanda. 

For more information, please visit [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angola|Wikipedia]].
